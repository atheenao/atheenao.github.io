Recipe #1: Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Makes: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Butter, softened 3/4 cup
Sugar 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp.
Brown Sugar 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp.
Vanilla 1/2 tbsp.
Eggs 1
Flour 2 cups
Baking Soda 1 tsp.
Salt 1/4 tsp.
Chocolate Chips 1 cup

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Stir together the flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl; set aside
  3. Cream together the butter, sugars, and eggs until fluffy
  4. Add in dry ingredients and mix well
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips
  6. Spoon onto an ungreased cookie sheet
  7. Bake for 11-13 minutes until golden brown then transfer to a cooling rack for 1-2 minutes

Recipe #2: Honey Soy Glazed Salmon

  • Time: 50 mins
  • Makes: 2 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Salmon
Skinless Salmon 12 oz.
Olive Oil 1 tbsp.
Honey Soy Marinade
Garlic, minced 4 cloves
Ginger, minced 2 tsp.
Red Pepper Flakes 1/2 tsp.
Olive Oil 1 tbsp.
Low Sodium Soy Sauce 1/3 cup
Honey 1/3 cup

Directions

  1. Place salmon in a sealable bag or medium bowl
  2. In a small bowl or measuring cup, mix marinade ingredients
  3. Pour half of the marinade on the salmon, and save the other half for later
  4. Let the salmon marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes
  5. In a medium pan, heat olive oil. Add the salmon to the pan, and save the leftover marinade. Cook salmon on one side for about 2-3 minutes then flip over and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  6. Remove salmon from pan and pour in remaining marinade to reduce
  7. Serve the salmon with sauce and a side of veggies

Source: Honey Glazed Salmon Recipe


Recipe #3: Garlic Bread

  • Time: 18 mins
  • Makes: 4-8 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Italian bread or French bread 1 loaf
Unsalted Butter 1 stick
Garlic, smashed and minced 2 cloves
Freshly Chopped Parsleys 1 large tbsp.
Parmesan Cheese 1/4 cup

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cut the loaf in half, horizontally, and mix the butter, garlic, and parsley together in a small bowl
  3. Spread butter mixture over the the two bread halves
  4. Place on a sturdy baking pan and heat for 10 minutes
  5. Remove from the oven and sprinkle on parmesan cheese, return to the oven on the highest rack
  6. Broil on high for 2-3 minutes or until the edges brown and the cheese starts to bubble
  7. Remove from the oven, let cool, and slice into 1-inch thick pieces

Source: Garlic Bread Recipe
